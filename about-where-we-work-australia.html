Paul Romeo

DIRECTOR OF THE BOARD SUKHI 222 AUSTRALIA

I started my working career as a scientist however transitioned out of the laboratory at a young age. Over 20+ years I have worked in commercial science and healthcare servicing a range of industries from hospitals, diagnostic pathology, research and academia, pharmaceutical, food, and environmental sectors. My responsibilities have spanned across account management, business development, sales management, and general management. I have successfully achieved and exceeded the expectations of the companies that I have worked with. I have done so by being driven by a passion to serve the customer to the highest standards with honesty and integrity. I have always had a thirst for knowledge and a passion for growing and progressing. In parallel to my career, I have had a keen interest in personal development. I have studied with the likes of Tony Robbins, Robert Kiyosaki, Dean Grazosi, Wim Hof, to name a few. I am a qualified life coach and NLP practitioner and have used these skills to both develop myself and assist others to be the best versions of themselves. In my view you are either progressing or decaying in life. So, whilst I walk this earth I would rather continue to keep moving forward and evolving.
